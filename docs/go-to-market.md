# Go-To-Market

## Positioning

SingCoach is an iOS vocal coaching app that records a short take, analyzes pitch and dynamics on-device, and returns specific AI coaching feedback.

## Best First Customer

- Singers practicing daily on iPhone.
- Beginner vocal students.
- Coaches who want quick student check-ins between lessons.

## Monetization Path

| Tier | Offer | Buyer |
|---|---|---|
| Free App | Limited analyses, basic score, local metrics. | Individual singers. |
| Pro Subscription | Unlimited session history, practice plans, exports, personalized drills. | Serious learners and creators. |
| Coach Mode | Student folders, assignments, weekly trend reports. | Vocal teachers. |
| School License | Multi-student dashboard and privacy controls. | Music schools. |

## Launch Hooks

1. "Record one take and know what to practice next."
2. "On-device pitch analysis, AI feedback, no audio sent for analysis."
3. "A vocal coach in your pocket between lessons."
